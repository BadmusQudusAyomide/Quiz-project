ELECTRICITY AND ELECTRIC FIELD

Electrostatics is a branch of science that deals with the phenomena that arises fros static electric charges. Charges were first grouped into positive and negative by Benjamin Franklin from year[1706-1709]
Like charges repel each other while unlike charges attract each other this can be seen by using simple scientific experiments if we rub a plastic ruler with fur and a glass rod with silk the plastic ruler will become negatively charged while the glass rod becomes positively charged so if we try to bring them together we will notice some sort of attraction between them

the law of conservation of electric charge states , that the net charge produced during any process is 'ZERO'
